ABOUT US

REGAL EVERSHINE, an Saudi Based company is one of the leading Dealer and distributor of Engineering Plastics and Speciality Polymers. Established in the year 2019, the company has its head office with well-equipped, state-of-the-art facility in Makka Street, Saudi Arabia with satellite offices at India.

As well as distribution points at various locations in the World including India, Saudi Arabia, Shanghai, NewYork, Yantain, Houston, Jakarta, and Busan. We have available EVA Product Grade - EVA 2518-CO, LD-800-OO, EVA-3522-CO, EVA-2014-CO, LD2023-CC and PBT Product - O.75 I.V, 0.85 I.V, 0.95 I.V, 0.65.I.V, 1.10 I.V .

By serving as a bridge between the polymers manufacturer and the Engineering Plastics industry, REGAL EVERSHINE is committed to serve its valued customers with the best quality, timely and cost-effective solutions to their requirements.

Applications

Our range of products find application in the production of apoxy bobbins, side mirrors, MCB, bumper and varied other plastic items and radiator fans. It is also used in film for packaging for food stuffs, profiles and strapping, components for hardware, building and furniture industries, mono filaments.

PBT Technology

PBT is a crystalline, high molecular weight polymer that has an excellent balance of properties and processing characteristics. Because the material crystallizes rapidly, mold cycles are short and molding temperatures can be lower than for many engineering plastics.

Market Sectors

PBT Granules. We are a leading Dealer, exporter and supplier of PBT Granules based in Saudi Arabia and India. The granules we offer are known for their strength, density and good resistance to chemicals.
